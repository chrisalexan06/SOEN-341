## Problem Statement
Maintaining a healthy lifestyle often involves tracking macros and managing recipes, but this can quickly become overwhelming. Without a unified solution, users are left juggling multiple apps and manual calculations, making it harder to stay consistent with their health goals. Common challenges include:
- Difficulty tracking which foods provide specific nutritional benefits
- Struggling to find recipes that are both healthy and enjoyable
- Lack of a centralized platform to manage weekly meal goals, recipes, and nutrition statistics

## About NutriFlow
NutriFlow is a web application designed to help students plan meals, manage recipes, and track healthy eating habits with ease.
#Core Features:

### Weekly Meal Planner:
- Visual weekly calendar displaying planned meals
- Automatic calculation of daily calories and macros
- Real-time progress tracking toward your nutritional goals

### Recipe Management
- Create, save, and organize custom recipes
- Pre-calculated macros stored with each recipe
- Easy drag-and-drop meal planning

### Progress Tracking
- Home page dashboard with progress bars
- Visual indicators showing goal achievement
- Centralized view of all nutrition statistics

### Recipe Discovery
- Community feed showcasing other users' recipes
- Nearby restaurant recommendations
- Find inspiration for your next meal

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: TypeScript, Next.js, Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Node.js (Server Actions)
- Database: PostgreSQL (Hosted on Supabase)
- ORM: Prisma
- Cloud Host: Vercel
- Authentication: Clerk
- Design: Figma
